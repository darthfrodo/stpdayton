Welcome!

St. Paul's is a parish of the Orthodox Church in America Diocese of the Midwest. Thanks for visiting our parish web page and for giving us the opportunity to introduce ourselves to you. Please take time to look around our web site and make yourself familiar with our parish community. Visitors and inquirers are welcomed and expected. Contact us if you have questions about Orthodoxy or about our parish. May God bless you always. See you at the church. -Fr. Ted
